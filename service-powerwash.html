Powerwashing at Leaf Cleaning

Nebraska weather is tough on exterior surfaces. Between hard winters, spring mud, summer heat, and fall leaves, your driveway, patio, deck, and siding accumulate years of grime that regular cleaning simply can't touch. At Leaf Cleaning, our professional powerwashing service blasts it all away — safely, effectively, and with the care your property deserves.

We use professional-grade pressure washing equipment calibrated to the right PSI for each surface. Concrete gets different treatment than wood decking, and painted siding gets a gentle soft wash instead of high pressure. We pre-treat surfaces with eco-friendly degreasers and algaecides, protect your landscaping, and document every job with before-and-after photos sent straight to your phone. When we're done, your property will look like new again.

Whether it's a one-time refresh or a recurring plan, Leaf Cleaning's powerwashing team brings consistency, expertise, and pride to every job. We serve homeowners and businesses across Kearney and Lincoln, NE — and we back every visit with our satisfaction guarantee.

  • Surface-safe technique — right PSI for every material, soft wash for delicate siding
  • Eco-friendly pre-treatment degreasers and algaecides

Our 4-Step Powerwashing Process

A systematic approach for maximum results on every surface

01

Assess Surface

We evaluate every surface — concrete, wood, siding, or pavers — to determine the right pressure, nozzle, and cleaning solution before we start.

02

Pre-Treat & Soak

We apply eco-friendly degreasers and algaecides to heavily stained areas, allowing them to break down grime and organic growth before washing begins.

03

Pressure Wash

Using calibrated PSI for each surface, we systematically power wash away years of stains, algae, oil, and grime — safely and thoroughly.

04

Rinse & Inspect

We do a final rinse and walk-through inspection to ensure every surface meets our standards, then send you before-and-after photos right to your phone.
