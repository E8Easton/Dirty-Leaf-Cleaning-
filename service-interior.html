Interior Window Cleaning at Leaf Cleaning

At Leaf Cleaning, we specialize in Interior Window Cleaning, delivering a spotless, streak-free finish on every pane inside your home. Our expert service removes fingerprints, dust, smudges, and hard water deposits — restoring crystal-clear views and bringing more natural light into your living spaces.

We use professional-grade, eco-friendly squeegees and cleaning solutions that are 100% safe for your family, pets, and hardwood floors. Our technicians lay down protective drop cloths before starting, work carefully around your furniture and décor, and leave no mess behind.

To complement our meticulous cleaning process, Leaf Cleaning offers flexible recurring interior window cleaning services. Our members enjoy consistently pristine windows inside their home all year round without the hassle of scheduling.

Our Interior Cleaning Process

Four careful steps for a spotless, residue-free finish inside your home

1
Dust & Prep
We lay down protective drop cloths and dust frames, sills, and blinds before any wet cleaning begins.
2
Clean & Scrub
Eco-safe solution is applied and each pane gently scrubbed to remove fingerprints, smudges, and buildup.
3
Squeegee
Professional squeegees draw every drop off the glass for a perfectly residue-free, streak-free finish.
4
Sill Detail
We wipe down every sill, frame, and edge — then remove our drop cloths and leave no trace behind.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about interior window cleaning

01

How often should interior windows be cleaned?

We recommend interior window cleaning 2–4 times per year, depending on your lifestyle. Homes with pets, kids, or allergies benefit most from more frequent visits. Our Quarterly plan is the perfect balance of value and care.

02

Do you use chemicals that are safe inside my home?

Yes — our interior cleaning solutions are 100% non-toxic, biodegradable, and safe for children, pets, and hardwood floors. We never use harsh chemicals indoors.

03

Do I need to be home for interior cleaning?

Yes — for interior window cleaning we do need access to your home. We'll work with your schedule to find a convenient time and our team is fully background-checked and uniformed.

04

Will you move furniture to clean the windows?

We'll carefully move lightweight items and lay down drop cloths to protect your floors and furniture. For large pieces, we'll work around them and ask you in advance if anything needs to be shifted.

05

What areas do you serve?

Leaf Cleaning proudly serves Kearney, Nebraska and Lincoln, Nebraska, including surrounding communities. Call us at (402) 309-0128 to confirm service in your area.
